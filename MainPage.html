Hi I'm Akimi!

Introduction

I’m a self-taught digital artist and writer, I use art and storytelling to bring ideas to life. My work is driven by curiosity and interests, blending colors, light, and words to create an immersive story. Through continuous learning and experimentation, I aim to inspire others to explore their own creativity.

About the Artist

I am currently honing my abilities in 3D environment design using Blender, continuously working on projects to enhance my craft. One of my notable works includes a 3D-rendered environment featuring an abandoned kingdom surrounded by a river and a vast field of flowers. I constantly explore new ideas, refining my expertise in design, art, and 3D world creation. With a strong foundation built from years of creative growth, I aim to leave my mark in the gaming and creative industries, bringing imaginative worlds to life for players to experience and enjoy.
